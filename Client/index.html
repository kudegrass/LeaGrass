About Grass.com

Welcome to Grass.com, your premier online review portal for the Licensure Examination for Agriculturists. Our platform provides comprehensive resources to help aspiring agriculturists succeed in their licensure exams and advance their careers.

Our platform offers:

  • Structured review materials for all six major subject areas
  • 100-question mock examinations for each subject area
  • Curated list of reliable online resources from international organizations
  • Progress tracking and performance analysis
  • Regularly updated content based on current agricultural practices and technologies

The mock examinations are designed to simulate the actual licensure exam format, helping you become familiar with the types of questions and time constraints you'll face. Each exam covers the full scope of knowledge required for that subject area.

At Grass.com, we're committed to providing high-quality educational resources that empower future agriculturists. Our name reflects our commitment to the foundation of agriculture - healthy grasslands, sustainable farming practices, and the growth of knowledge in the agricultural sciences.

This portal is continuously updated to reflect the latest developments in agricultural science and practice, ensuring that you're studying the most current information available.
